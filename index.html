Legal Practice

CORPORATE AND COMMERCIAL MATTERS

Setting up of new companies in Hong Kong, Mainland China (including set-up of representative offices, etc.) and overseas; serving as the long-term legal advisor for companies with legal services rendered including drafting and reviewing of commercial documents and issuance of legal opinions and legal due diligence reports; advising and acting in the matter of acquisitions of shares or equity interest or businesses or of capital re-structure of limited companies etc.

DEALING OF REAL ESTATE AND BUILDING PROJECT DEVELOPMENT

Specialized in handling building project developments of real estate including land acquisition by way of compulsory sale in Hong Kong; also experienced in conveyancing property transactions (including sale of first-hand properties) and/or leasing matters, etc.

LISTING APPLICATIONS AND FINANCIAL FUNDING ARRANGEMENT

Arrangement for enterprises in Mainland China to apply (with us mostly acting as lawyers for the company) for listing in Hong Kong or in other jurisdictions, including London (LSE and AIM markets), Frankfurt and Singapore in co-operation with associated financial advisor, accountants and sponsors; arrangement of loans or project finance or corporate restructuring for enterprises with good forecast; advising on setting up Limited Partnership Funds etc.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Local (Hong Kong) and world-wide registrations; drafting of assignments or licence agreements and offering legal advice.

CIVIL & COMMERCIAL LITIGATION & ARBITRATION

Handling civil and commercial litigation and arbitration Hong Kong, particularly in handling cases regarding corporate, commercial, and real estate laws; also assisting or engaging PRC lawyers to deal with cross-border commercial litigation and arbitration, and representing our clients in enforcement of judgments issued by Mainland China Courts or by Hong Kong Courts.

INHERITANCE

Handling the formalities of inheritance and applying for grant of legal representation by the court, including provision of legal opinions regarding administration and distribution of estate.

NOTARIZATION

Dealing with attesting affairs between Hong Kong and Mainland China, including those required by CEPA and notarization and authentication of documents for use worldwide.
